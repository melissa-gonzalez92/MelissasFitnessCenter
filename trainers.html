Meet Our Trainers

Anya Petrova

Specialty: Strength Training & Powerlifting

Olympic Weightlifting: Anya is a certified Olympic Weightlifting coach with 10 years of experience. She specializes in proper form, technique refinement, and strength development for competitive and recreational lifters. She also incorporates injury prevention techniques into her training programs.

Powerlifting: Anya also has extensive experience in powerlifting, focusing on maximal strength gains in the squat, bench press, and deadlift. She tailors powerlifting programs to individual needs and goals, emphasizing progressive overload and personalized training plans.

Strength and Conditioning: Beyond specialized lifting, Anya is a skilled strength and conditioning coach, designing programs for athletes in various sports to improve performance and prevent injuries. Her programs often integrate plyometrics, speed drills, and flexibility work.

Certifications:
  • USA Weightlifting Level 1 Coach
  • NSCA Certified Strength and Conditioning Specialist (CSCS)
  • CrossFit Level 1 Trainer
  • CPR and First Aid Certified
